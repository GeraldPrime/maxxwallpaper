About Us

Welcome to Maxx Wallpaper & Décor Ltd

We are team based in Nigeria. Our expertise on House Design are top notch. Our values include:

  • Smart & Unique Wall Work

  • The Best Quality Standards

Maxx Wallpaper & Décor Ltd is one of the foremost and fastest growing interior décor firm in Nigeria with over a decade experience in Interior Decoration and related services, this makes her one of the most sought after firm in sales and installation of all shades of wall coverings such as; wallpapers, fluted panels, PU stone panels, window blinds, curtains. All kinds of wood finish flooring such as; SPC floor, Vinyl PVC floors, laminate floors etc. in Nigeria. The company is headquartered in the heart of the Federal Capital Territory Abuja Wuse 2 District with branch offices in Lekki, Lagos State and Suleja town of Niger State.
